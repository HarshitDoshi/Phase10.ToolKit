Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Phase10.ToolKit app, which is developed and operated by shunyaek.

What data do we collect?

We collect the following data from users of our app:

  • Device information, such as the device's model, operating system, and unique identifier.
  • Usage data, such as the app's features that users have used and the frequency with which they have used them.
  • Crash reports, which are automatically generated when the app crashes.

How do we use your data?

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the app.
  • Analyze how users use the app.
  • Detect and fix bugs.
  • Send you marketing messages, if you have opted in to receive them.

Who do we share your data with?

We may share your data with third-party partners who help us provide and improve the app. These partners may include analytics providers, advertising networks, and crash reporting services.

How do we protect your data?

We take steps to protect your data, such as using encryption and security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your data. You also have the right to opt out of receiving marketing messages.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at 01@shunyaek.se.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
